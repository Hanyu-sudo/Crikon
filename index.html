  • Crikon

    Crikon is a treat for medium-sized dogs that is made of cricket and a flavor of bacon. The crickets provide proteins that give amino acids and vitamins B2 and B12 that give the dog better brain function and better production of energy and calcium that gives the dog a better development of the bones.

  • Purpose

    The purpose of this project is to the animals to have a better lifestyle healthy especially for the dogs, but all animals can consume it to be healthier and have more energy.

    This project will not only be positive in your pets health, but it is also a delicious way for them to stay healthy as crickets provide proteins.

    Ingredients

    Bacon, cricket, coconut oil, mass, water.

    Procedure

    You smash the cricket into tiny pieces, then you cook the bacon and cut it, then joined with water and a litle bit of mass, and the last step is cooked in the oven.
